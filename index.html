Our Story

Why Play?

Play is not just a padel and pickleball club, but an indoor sporting brand taking over the UK. Play is about human connection, experiences, and playfulness. As human connection is becoming more important now than ever before, we are on a mission to create a place where people come to meet and make new friends, stay fit, and create experiences.

Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or competing at a regional level, our coaches, community, and courts are here for you. All abilities, all ages, all welcome.

If you're bored, need to get-out when it's raining, looking for a human connection, or just want to get off your phone, come and play!

We Are Raising

We are currently raising our seed round to launch our ambitious new indoor sporting brand in the UK, built around community, connection, and high-quality experiences. Our mission is simple: to create a welcoming space where people come to meet, make new friends, stay fit, and build lasting memories. By combining the fast-growing sports of padel and pickleball with a vibrant social atmosphere, we are redefining what a modern sports club can be.

Our first flagship club will open in Winchester, bringing premium indoor courts, expertly delivered programming, and a carefully designed social environment under one roof. We see strong demand for accessible, social fitness experiences that go beyond traditional gyms. With early momentum building and a clear expansion roadmap, this seed round will fund site launch, brand development, and operational setup—positioning us to scale a community-driven sporting concept across the UK.
