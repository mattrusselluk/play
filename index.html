Our Story

Padel for
Everyone

Play was founded by a group of Winchester locals who fell in love with padel on a trip to Spain. We brought the sport home — and built something truly special in the heart of Hampshire.

Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or competing at a regional level, our coaches, community, and courts are here for you. All abilities, all ages, all welcome.
